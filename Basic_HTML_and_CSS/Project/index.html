Moon Info!

The USA and NASA landed on the moon in 1969!

NASA

NASA is the United State's space agency. NASA stands for National Aeronautics and Space Adminstration.

Moon Landing 1969

NASA landed on the moon in 1969. Neil Armstrong, Buzz Aldrin and Michael Collins were the three NASA astronauts to complete this mission

The exact date of this moon landing was July 20, 1969.
